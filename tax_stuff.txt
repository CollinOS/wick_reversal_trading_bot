US TAX TREATMENT SUMMARY

Perpetual Futures Trades

Each position open to close is a taxable event.
Gains and losses are treated as capital gains.
Almost all trades will be short-term.
Short-term capital gains are taxed at ordinary income rates.

Funding Payments

Positive funding payments are ordinary income.
Negative funding payments are ordinary expenses.
Funding payments should NOT be included in capital gains calculations.

Tax Reporting Responsibility

Hyperliquid does not issue Form 1099.
All activity must be self-reported.

TRANSACTION CATEGORIZATION RULES

Perpetual futures trades:
Report as capital gains or losses.

Funding payments:
Report as ordinary income or ordinary expense.

Wallet transfers:
Transfers between your wallet and Hyperliquid are non-taxable.
Incorrectly labeling transfers can create false taxable gains.
