Running live_monitor.py along with run_paper_trade.py

  Terminal 1 - Live Monitor (detects opportunities):
  python live_monitor.py --current TAO-PERP AAVE-PERP ZRO-PERP --auto-update --output active_symbols.json --interval 300

  Terminal 2 - Paper Trading Bot (executes trades):
  python run_paper_trade.py --private-key YOUR_KEY --watch-symbols active_symbols.json

  What Happens When Symbols Change

  1. Monitor detects kPEPE-PERP has score 65 (better than your worst symbol at 40)
  2. Monitor writes new symbols to active_symbols.json
  3. Bot detects file change within 30 seconds
  4. Bot prints update notification:
  ============================================================
  ğŸ”„ SYMBOL UPDATE DETECTED
  ============================================================
     â• Adding: kPEPE-PERP
     â– Removing: ZRO-PERP
     ğŸ“Š New symbols: TAO-PERP, AAVE-PERP, kPEPE-PERP
  ============================================================
  5. Bot automatically starts streaming new symbol, stops old one
  6. Existing positions are kept until they close naturally
